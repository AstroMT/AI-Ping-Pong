AI Ping Pong Game




Instructions

1. Keep your laptop screen straight
2. Move yourself approximately 3 to 4 feet away from the laptop
3. Move your right wrist in an ascending and descending manner (a red dot should appear on your right wrist)
4. Now press the play button, and again move yourself 3-4 feet away from the laptop
5. The red paddle is your paddle, it will move as per the movement of your right wrist
